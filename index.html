History of BCA Department

A journey of excellence, innovation, and academic achievement since inception.

The Department of Computer Applications was established in 1998 with well-equipped facilities and dedicated teaching faculty. In its formative years, the department was headed by Mr. Sathyanarayanan and later by Mr. R. T. Ashok Kumar until 2006, laying a strong foundation for academic growth and skill-based learning. From 2007 to 2021, the department was led by Dr. P. S. S. Akilashri, who introduced several academic enrichments, strengthened the curriculum, and expanded the department's focus towards industry-aligned training and research culture. From 2022 onwards, the department has been efficiently headed by Dr. M. Anusha, under whose leadership the department continues to grow with modernized lab infrastructures, updated syllabus frameworks, and enhanced student development initiatives. The BCA programme offered by our institution has been approved by AICTE from 2025 onwards.

The department's curriculum is designed to impart concepts of Computer Science and Applications, blending theoretical knowledge with intensive hands-on training. Students are encouraged to explore real-time IT industry challenges through internships, mini-projects, major projects, and technical workshops. The syllabus ensures students gain the required competency for competitive examinations, placements, higher studies, and professional skill development.

Faculty Team

The department comprises a team of committed faculty members: Dr. M. Anusha (Head), S. Revathi, R. Kevin Joshua, B. Nagajothi, Dr. G. Srinaganya, A. Aasmi Sabana, R. Anitha, S. Ruby, V. K. Giridharan, and G. Fathima Haseen Raihana. Each faculty member specializes in industry-relevant domains such as Big Data Analytics, Cloud Computing, Evolutionary Clustering, Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, and other emerging IT technologies. To stay updated with advancements in automation, all staff members have attended Robotic Process Automation (RPA) FDP, which encouraged the department to assign RPA-based group projects for students, enabling them to work in teams and gain practical exposure to automation tools.

Research & Innovation

The department has guided 7 Ph.D. scholars, among whom 3 scholars have successfully completed their theses, reflecting the department's growing research strength. The department has also conducted the Smart India Hackathon, where most of the participating teams from our college were selected, showcasing the innovation and problem-solving skills of the students.

Placements & Alumni Support

Many of our students have been placed as interns in reputed companies, gaining valuable industry exposure. In addition, our alumni actively mentor the current students through project guidance and internships, contributing significantly to their academic and career growth.

Commitment to Excellence

Through continuous curriculum enhancement, ICT-enabled teaching, coding clubs, research mentoring, industry seminars, and project expos, the Department of Computer Applications remains dynamic, future-ready, and dedicated to producing competent IT professionals.
